Welcome

Data and AI platforms that feel less like a fight.

Hi, I'm Shubham. I build and operate large-scale data and AI/ML platforms that support data engineers, data scientists, and analytics teams across enterprise environments.

My work sits at the intersection of platform engineering, system administration, data science tooling, and enterprise operations. I focus on making platforms like Posit/RStudio, SAS, JupyterHub, Databricks, Dataiku, and Tableau reliable, secure, automated, and genuinely pleasant to use.

Under the hood, that means working across Linux and Windows systems, access control, upgrades, monitoring, automation, scripting, troubleshooting, integrations, and the operational details that help teams spend less time fighting infrastructure and more time delivering value.
